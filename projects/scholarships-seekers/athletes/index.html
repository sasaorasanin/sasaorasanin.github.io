What is the best way to possibly get scholarship?

Let's divide the presentation of yourself into a few parts:

  1. Your videos are the main point of your self presentation because the coach must see how do you play and think about game, so not only highlights but also full games and training videos are desirable.
  2. Most likely every video can be faked and because of that the coach may still skip up your presentation, therefore you should do a little research about his team, their stats and explain why do you think that your position at that team would change things for better.
  3. Studies are also important for coaches as well because you cannot get scholarship if your GPA score and/or TOEFL/SAT/ACT tests has less than minimum required by their school.
  4. Your sports CV should have a clean self photo, full name, birthday date, where are you from, your phone contact, your GPA score and other test scores if you took them. Your player description as height, weight, dress number, game position, position percentage etc... And most importantly list of your videos
  5. Athough, the age limitations are not presented at these schools websites. Every sport should 100% give scholarships to athletes under 21 years old.

I put this out there just to make sure you understood that doing all this may still be unsuccessful if coach is not seeing your email, so don't spam messages but stay being persistant by sending emails daily just in case coach skipped your email without noticing.
