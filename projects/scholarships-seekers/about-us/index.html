You should never let anyone decide things out for you, unless the agency that is presenting you is globally accepted and verified. Otherwise you could end up like most of us who let some random agencies decide where we could go, which in 99% of time ended up unsuccessfully.

Therefor you should either use this free list and find coaches emails yourself, ask them for scholarships and be persistent.

Or buy package with all emails or emails from colleges / universities that you choosed, save up a lot time from searching and just keep asking.

Either way you should be the one that decides your future carrer!
